Visit Southern Illinois

Enjoy history and nature in this quiet nook of the Midwest.

Follow the steps of President Lincoln

Follow the painted footsteps of Lincoln down to the site of the third Lincoln-Douglas debate, where there are statues and a plaque commemorating the occasion.

Visit the bluffs of Giant City State Park

Hike the trails over and under the bluffs of Giant City in Makanda, IL. Be sure to also visit the rustic Giant City Lodge built in the 1930's!

Explore the wineries

If there is one reason people visit Southern Illinois it's for the beautiful wineries. Reserve a day in your visit for some winery-hopping you you won't regret it.
